Awesome! Your environment is ready. Let's tackle Day 1: Parsing and Chunking Documents.

Today, we are going to write a clean, isolated Python script that takes a raw PDF or text file, reads it, and chops it into structured chunks.

Why do we "chunk" documents?
Large Language Models (LLMs) can only read a certain number of words at a time. If you feed an entire 100-page manual into an LLM all at once, it costs a lot of money, slows down the response time, and the AI often forgets details hidden in the middle.

By chopping the document into smaller paragraphs (chunks), our search engine can look at our user's question, find the exact 3 or 4 relevant paragraphs, and only send those to the AI.
